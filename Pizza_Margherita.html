Pizza Margherita

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (usually around 500°F or 260°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle on a floured surface.
  3. Place the dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Slice the tomatoes and arrange them evenly on the dough.
  5. Tear the fresh mozzarella into pieces and distribute it over the tomatoes.
  6. Sprinkle some salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  7. Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the pizza.
  8. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves before serving.
